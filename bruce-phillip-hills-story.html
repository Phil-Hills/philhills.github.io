Career Story of Bruce Phillip Hills (Professional Edition)

The career story of Bruce Phillip Hills, professionally known today as Phil Hills, is one defined by curiosity, reinvention, and the pursuit of intelligent automation.

Today, Phil designs and builds intelligent systems that move the world toward more efficient, AI-first operations.
