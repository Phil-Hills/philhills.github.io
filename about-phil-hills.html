About Phil Hills — AI Programmer & Automation Architect in Seattle

Phil Hills is an AI programmer, builder, and automation architect based in Seattle, Washington. He focuses on creating intelligent systems that solve real-world operational problems using machine learning, workflow automation, and custom-built technology.

Phil specializes in designing AI-first solutions that combine pattern recognition, reasoning systems, and automated processes. His work spans intelligent communication pipelines, data-driven decision systems, and the emerging domain of protocol-based AI agents — including his flagship project, Cube Protocol.

With years of hands-on experience in mortgage technology, marketing automation, and high-volume operational systems, Phil brings a uniquely practical approach to AI engineering. He builds tools that reduce friction, increase efficiency, and empower people to work smarter with less effort.
