Queen Anne – a Seattle neighborhood perspective

A high-level, marketing-focused look at Queen Anne — its hilltop charm, scenic views, and the types of buyers who often explore this iconic Seattle neighborhood.

Seattle, Washington Historic homes & scenic overlooks Urban village with neighborhood feel

Who Queen Anne often attracts

Queen Anne is known for its historic architecture, tree-lined streets, and panoramic views of downtown Seattle, Elliott Bay, and the Olympic Mountains. The neighborhood tends to attract professionals, long-term residents, downsizers, and people who value a strong sense of community.

Housing varies from classic single-family homes to condos and low-rise buildings, with a vibrant commercial district centered along Queen Anne Avenue.
