Clair Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

Introduction

Clair ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Clair Chrome Extension.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Conversation History: Messages you send to Clair and responses from AI agents
  • Page Context: When you request help, we access the current page content you're viewing
  • User Preferences: Settings and configurations you choose

Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the extension (features used, frequency)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, error logs

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To deliver AI assistance and remember conversation context
  • Improve Experience: To enhance features and fix bugs
  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: All data is stored in Google Cloud Storage (GCS) with encryption at rest
  • Compression: Conversations are compressed using CUBE Protocol for efficiency
  • Access Control: Data is associated with your Google account and only accessible by you
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Google Cloud Platform (for storage), Stripe (for payments)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your stored conversations
  • Export: Download your data in JSON format
  • Delete: Permanently delete all your data at any time
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the extension to stop data collection

Data Retention

  • Active Users: Conversations stored indefinitely for memory functionality
  • Deleted Data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request
  • Inactive Accounts: Data retained for 2 years, then automatically deleted

Chrome Extension Permissions

Clair requests the following permissions:

  • storage: To save conversation history and preferences
  • activeTab: To access page content when you request help
  • identity: To associate data with your Google account
  • scripting: To inject the Clair interface on web pages

Third-Party Services

  • Google Cloud Platform: Data storage and AI processing
  • Stripe: Payment processing (we do not store credit card information)
  • Google Gemini: AI language model for responses

Children's Privacy

Clair is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the extension or email.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

  • Legal Basis: Consent and legitimate interest
  • Data Controller: Phil Hills, Seattle, WA, USA
  • EU Representative: Contact us for details
  • Right to Complain: You may file a complaint with your local data protection authority

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
