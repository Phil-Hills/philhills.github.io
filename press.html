Professional Bio (Long)

Phil Hills is a systems-focused AI programmer specializing in intelligent automation, machine learning engineering, and protocol-based multi-agent design.

Over his career, Phil has built AI tools for communications systems, workflow automation platforms, predictive intelligence, and operational decision engines. His flagship project is Cube Protocol, a modular framework for building reasoning-driven automation across complex workflows.

Phil’s engineering philosophy centers around clarity, transparency, and human-augmenting automation. He believes AI should reduce friction, amplify human capability, and make sophisticated operations accessible to everyone.

Phil lives and works in Seattle, WA, and publishes his research, technical deep dives, and engineering worklogs on PhilHills.com and PhilHills.ai.
